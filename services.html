Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book? +
We recommend booking as early as November to guarantee your preferred slot. The mid-December periods are the most requested.
How long does the installation take? +
Between 2 and 4 hours depending on the chosen service. For complex exterior installations, allow a full day.
Do you offer removal after the holidays? +
Yes, removal is included in all our packs and services. We generally intervene in early January.
Can we customize the decorations? +
Absolutely! We adapt our creations to your favorite colors, your style (traditional, modern, Scandinavian...), and your wishes. A consultation is included in our premium packs.
Do you operate outside of Paris? +
Yes, we cover the entire Île-de-France region. Travel fees may apply beyond 50km from Paris. Contact us for a custom quote.
